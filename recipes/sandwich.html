Sandwich Recipe

The following is the most basic recipe ever for making a sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese slices

Steps

  1. Butter the bread.
  2. Heat both sides of the bread on medium heat.
  3. When both sides are slightly darkened, add cheese slices.
  4. Put together bread with cheese in the middle and stop heating once it's melted.
